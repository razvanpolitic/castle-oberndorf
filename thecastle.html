Vicinity

The Oberndorf Castle impresses with its unique location in the midst of nature in the idyllic Lech landscape.

Meadows, 3 hectares of fields and forest surround the castle which is not open to the public, and you can celebrate weddings, engagements, birthdays, communions, company events and much more exclusively, in a private ambience.

Our Rooms

At Oberndorf Castle, you have at your disposal fairytale-like and lovingly designed rooms for the special moments in your life. On your big day, we only rent our premises to you, so that you can celebrate with your guests exclusively and in a private atmosphere!

Marstall

The stables are connected to the St. George Hall by a wide passage. It serves as a dance and party area. On cold days you can make yourself comfortable by the fireplace and enjoy pure romance. From the Marstall you can reach our beautiful and large terrace with a fabulous view of the castle park.

St. Georg-Saal

The St. George Hall impresses with its imposing column and vaulted construction. It can be seated for around 120 people.

Wintergarden

You can rent the palace park, which will enchant you with its old trees and a sea of flowers, as well as the winter garden. Here you can organize your wedding ceremony, champagne reception as well as coffee and cake while enjoying nature to the fullest. You can use the conservatory all year round due to its heatability and enjoy the enchanted winter landscape.
